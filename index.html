Why i love Marvel

I love Marvel because I've always wanted to be a superhero. It's a small and yet big and fascinating world. It's also a lot of fun to make up predictions about the characters. But it also connects me to a good time with a classmate. Simply an incredible universe!

Why this Website

The reason for creating this website is that I want to find people who are also interested in this topic. They can join my Discord server. This way new contacts can be made and maybe a community meeting can be arranged to watch a new film in the cinema.
